News and Announcements

Are you looking for some helpful statistical resources to guide you through your data analysis? 

Look no further than the Statistical Consulting Unit’s revamped Statistical Resources page. This Resources page is a one stop shop for information about the Statistical Consulting Unit - who they are, the services they offer, their statistical workshops with workflows, and other helpful references on statistical theory and software.
The team has developed statistical workflows ranging from Linear models (e.g. ANOVA, ANCOVA, linear and logistic regression and model building), to Experimental Design, Sample Size Calculations, Meta-Analysis, Survival Analysis and Multivariate Analysis.
Check out their Statistical Resources page now! 

Training and Events

Cloud Computing at Sydney – Ronin Reasearch Cloud

31 January: Zoom

Not enough grunt in your laptop? Need a Linux/Windows machine to run a workflow? Artemis not fit-for purpose? Want unfettered access to unlimited GPU, CPU, RAM, and storage resources? 
Consider the Ronin Research Cloud, Sydney’s secure gateway to Amazon Web Services. 
Come and learn about the use-cases, costs, and general cloud computing concepts at our workshop on 31 January 2024. 
Registrations open on 15 December 2023! 

Foundational statistical workshops - planning your research

7 February Hybrid workshops: in-person or Zoom 

To support you with planning your research studies we are offering a full day of our 'foundation' workshops. They are running on Wednesday 7 February 2024 at Camperdown campus (venue TBC) and via Zoom. Attend “Research Essentials”, “Experimental Design” and "Power and Sample Size" to plan your study and set yourself up for research success!
Registrations open on 8 December 2023.
Don’t forget to check out our training calendar in the new year for more training opportunities.
